Prediction Market Proxy

Serverless proxy for Polymarket, Kalshi, SX Bet, Manifold, and controlled generic targets.

Health: /api/health

Polymarket: /api/polymarket/markets?limit=100

Kalshi: /api/kalshi/markets?limit=100

Generic: /api/proxy?target=polymarket&path=/markets&limit=100

Deployment instructions live in HELP.md in the repo.
